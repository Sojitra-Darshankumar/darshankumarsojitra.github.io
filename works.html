Project : Website

Based : Hospital Management System

Langages : Python, Django

Preview : https://empathygeneral.pythonanywhere.com

Welcome to Empathy General Hospital, where state-of-the-art technology intersects with compassionate healthcare. Our Hospital Management System website, intricately constructed using Python alongside the Django framework, redefines healthcare administration through the seamless amalgamation of three vital modules: Client (Patient/Appointment), Admin, and Doctor.

In the Client module, patients navigate through an intuitive interface to schedule appointments with their preferred physicians. Upon reservation, appointments seamlessly transmit to the designated doctor for confirmation, fostering efficient communication between patients and healthcare practitioners. Patients then embark on their in-person consultations, receiving bespoke treatment tailored to their unique requirements. Upon conclusion, patients transition to the Admin module for billing, where charges are meticulously calculated, encompassing room fees for admissions and medical services as per the administrator's discretion. Medication expenses are also accounted for, mirroring the patient's individualized treatment trajectory.

In the realm of administration, Empathy General Hospital ensures streamlined operations while upholding the utmost standards of patient care. Administrators concentrate solely on billing endeavors, meticulously crafting final invoices that encompass all incurred expenses. By delegating other administrative duties, administrators dedicate undivided focus to guaranteeing a seamless billing process for patients, reaffirming our unwavering dedication to empathy-infused healthcare.
